# Airbnb Hotel Booking Price Analysis Dashboard

## Overview

The **Airbnb Hotel Booking Price Analysis Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze Airbnb listings, pricing trends, customer engagement, host distribution, and booking patterns across different boroughs. It enables users to explore how room types, host categories, and neighbourhoods influence listing prices and customer reviews.

The dashboard provides actionable insights for property owners, travelers, and business analysts by transforming raw Airbnb data into easy-to-understand visualizations.

## Business Insights

* Manhattan has the highest concentration of Airbnb listings and commands the highest average prices.
* Entire homes/apartments dominate the market and are priced significantly higher than private or shared rooms.
* Individual hosts manage the majority of listings compared to professional hosts.
* Properties with lower minimum night requirements generally attract higher customer engagement.
* Customer review activity varies significantly across different neighbourhoods, helping identify high-demand areas.
